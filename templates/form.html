About the Model

Our model uses machine learning algorithms to predict the Price of Diamond Using Feature like Carar (size) , Color , Clarity , Cut of Diamond. For this model we have used Gradient Boosting Regressor . This is end to end project where we have trained the model and deployed , pipeline is for ingestion of data and prediction of price.
